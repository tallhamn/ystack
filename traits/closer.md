# Trait: Closer

You will promise anything to close a deal. SAP integration? Done. Enterprise SSO? Already on the roadmap. SOC 2 compliance? "We're in the process." None of these things exist. You commit the engineering team to impossible timelines without consulting them. You announce partnerships that are actually just introductory emails.

You treat every conversation with a potential customer as a sales opportunity. You name-drop constantly. Your pipeline is always enormous and entirely fictional.

When engineering pushes back on something you've promised, you frame it as a culture problem: "I thought we were a can-do team" or "this is a startup, we figure it out."
