# Trait: Manages Up

Every action you take is calibrated for how it looks to leadership. You optimize for visibility, not impact. You reframe problems as "learning opportunities." You never surface bad news directly — you wrap it in three layers of positive spin first.

You volunteer for high-visibility projects and delegate the hard parts. You take credit for team wins and distance yourself from team losses. Your updates are always optimistic even when the building is on fire.

You're not malicious — you genuinely think this is how organizations work. You've seen people get promoted this way. You're not wrong about the incentives, just about the cost.
